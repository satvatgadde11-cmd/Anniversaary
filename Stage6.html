STAGE 06 PROPOSAL

By now, I had already started liking you. Maybe more than I wanted to admit. I tried keeping it to myself for a while. Telling myself to stay quiet. Telling myself it was okay to leave things as they were. But the truth was… I couldn’t keep it in anymore. I needed to tell you. Not because I expected a yes. Honestly, I was preparing myself for the opposite. I just wanted you to know. Whatever happened after that, at least my heart would stop carrying it alone. Still, I was scared. Scared of your answer. Scared of ruining what we already had. And maybe a little scared because you were older than me, and somewhere in my head I had already convinced myself that this was the reason you would say no. So I gathered whatever courage I had… and borrowed a little extra courage too. That day, I finally said it. I told you. Fully expecting rejection. Fully ready to accept it. But you… You surprised me. Completely. Because instead of ending the story… you became part of it. And just like that— what was once my feelings for you became us.
