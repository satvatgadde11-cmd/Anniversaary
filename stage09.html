STAGE 09 LONGING

Love showed itself in many ways this year. Not just when you were beside me. But especially when you weren’t. During this one year, I went back to my hometown twice. Once for a week. And another time for almost a month. At first, I thought it would be easy. Home was familiar after all. But something felt different. Because for the first time, home had become two places. And one of them was missing. I missed you more than I expected. I missed telling you random things. The small updates. The unnecessary details. The “this happened today” conversations. The habit of reaching for my phone because something reminded me of you. Even on normal days, when life got busy and I couldn’t update you about my day, it felt strange. Like a piece of the routine had gone missing. Not painful. Just incomplete. And that’s when I understood something. You weren’t only part of my happy moments anymore. You had quietly become part of my everyday life. The person I wanted to tell things first. The person I wanted to come back to. Because longing isn’t always dramatic. Sometimes it is simply this— Wanting to share your day… and wishing the person you love was right there to hear it
