STAGE 01 UNKNOWN

Two people, living in different worlds, carrying different dreams, waking up to different mornings.

One had her eyes fixed on the future she wanted to build, the other was busy chasing his own path, both unaware that life had already started writing a chapter neither of them could see yet.

Days passed like ordinary days do. Classes, responsibilities, ambitions, little victories, silent struggles. Heads down. Focused on work. Moving through life as if it was only their story.

They did not know that somewhere ahead, their timelines were slowly moving toward the same point.

At that moment, there was no us. No memories. No nicknames. No late-night talks.

Just two people… Completely unaware that someone was already on their way to becoming home.
