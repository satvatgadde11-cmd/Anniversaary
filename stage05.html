STAGE 05-COMFORT

Somewhere between all the conversations, something quietly changed.

The days we talked felt normal.

The days we didn’t… felt strange.

I had gotten so used to your presence that not talking to you almost felt incomplete. Not because we had to talk, but because somewhere along the way, it had become my favourite part of the day.

There was comfort in it.
You ranting about work after a long day. Me complaining about exams and everything they brought with them. Laughing at things that made absolutely no sense to anyone else. Turning ordinary moments into something worth remembering.

Nothing extraordinary was happening.

No grand gestures.

Just two people slowly becoming a safe place for each other.

And maybe that’s when I realised… Home doesn’t always look like a place.

Sometimes it sounds like a familiar voice saying, “How was your day?”
