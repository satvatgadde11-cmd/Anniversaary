STAGE 12-HOME

There are versions of this world where we never met.
Worlds where I never walked into MSK-2 OPD that day.
Worlds where we remained only a senior and a junior.
Worlds where I never asked you for that movie.
Worlds where I stayed quiet.
Worlds where fear won.
Worlds where timing failed.
Worlds where distance became bigger than feelings.
Worlds where Guggi never existed.
Maybe those worlds are real somewhere.
Maybe they aren’t.
But I know one thing.
I am grateful this wasn’t one of them.
Because in this world… I met you.
I got to know you.
I laughed with you.
Missed you.
Chose you.
Grew with you.
And somewhere in between all of that… I found home.
Not a place.
Not a city.
Not even a moment.
A person.
You.
Thank you for this year.
For the memories.
For the chaos.
For staying.
For becoming one of the most beautiful parts of my life.
And if life allows me… I would still choose this universe again.
The one where you exist.
Happy First Anniversary, Guggi ❤️
