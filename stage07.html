STAGE 07 LITTLE THINGS

After that day, life didn’t suddenly become perfect. There were no dramatic changes. No movie montage moments. Instead… there were little things. The kind people usually overlook. Nicknames that started meaning more than actual names. Random conversations that went absolutely nowhere but somehow became the best part of the day. Laughing at things that made no sense. Sending things just because “this reminded me of you.” Getting used to each other’s habits. Learning moods without words. Creating a language that belonged only to us. And somewhere in all of that… you became Guggi. Not just a nickname. A feeling. A comfort. A small world I never knew I would build with someone. This stage wasn’t about grand gestures. It was about ordinary moments quietly turning into memories. Because in the end… love didn’t grow during the big moments. It grew in the tiny ones. The ones nobody notices. The ones I never want to forget.
