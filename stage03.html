STAGE 03 CURIOSITY

What started inside the walls of an OPD slowly refused to stay there. The conversations that once revolved only around cases, assessments and patients began stretching a little longer. Small interactions turned into familiar ones. Replies became easier. Presence became noticeable. Nothing had a name yet. We were still just a senior and a junior, moving through ordinary days, doing ordinary work. But somewhere between those routine conversations, curiosity quietly walked in. You stopped being someone I met in MSK-2 OPD. You became someone I looked forward to talking to. Not because I had to. Because I wanted to. It still wasn’t love. Not even close. Just a quiet feeling that said— “There’s something different here.”
