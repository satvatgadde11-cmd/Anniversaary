STAGE 10-GROWTH

This year didn’t just change us.

It changed me.

Somewhere between all the conversations, memories, chaos, comfort, and distance… I realised we weren’t the same people who met in MSK-2 OPD anymore.

We had grown.

You were building your path, carrying responsibilities, handling work, chasing the future you wanted.

I was fighting my own battles too — exams, responsibilities, figuring life out one step at a time.

And yet… somehow we kept finding our way back to each other.

There were days when life was heavy.

Days when one of us had less energy.

Days when things outside the relationship felt bigger than the relationship itself.

But instead of pulling away… we learned how to stand beside each other.

Not perfectly.

Just honestly.

You saw versions of me that were stressed, confused, tired, overthinking.

And still… you stayed.

And I got to watch you grow too.

Stronger.

More determined.

More you.

Maybe that’s what this stage was really about.

Not becoming perfect.

But becoming better.

Together.

Because love didn’t stop our growth.

It grew with us.

And somewhere along the way, you and me slowly became a team.
