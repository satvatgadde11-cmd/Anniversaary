STAGE 11 FUTURE

Somewhere along this journey, I stopped imagining only my future. Without realising it… it slowly started becoming ours. The dreams changed. The picture changed. And somehow, you were there in all of it. In the mountains we still have to visit. In the trips we keep talking about. In the versions of ourselves we haven’t met yet. You chasing your dreams. Me chasing mine. Still coming back to each other at the end of the day. I started imagining older versions of us. More mature. More tired maybe. Still laughing at stupid things. Still arguing over random topics. Still choosing each other. There are so many things we haven’t done yet. Places we haven’t seen. Memories we haven’t made. Stories we haven’t lived. And somehow that doesn’t scare me. It excites me. Because for the first time… the future doesn’t feel like a lonely road. It feels like a journey. And when I look ahead now, I don’t just see destinations. I see a hand I hope is still there. Yours. Because somewhere along the way… tomorrow quietly became “we.”
