STAGE 08-CHAOS

Somewhere along the way, we reached the part every story quietly hides.

The imperfect part.

The part where emotions became stronger, expectations became real, and little things sometimes felt bigger than they were.

There were misunderstandings.

Moments of silence.

Times when words didn’t come out the way we meant them.

Times when hurt sat between us even when neither of us wanted it there.

And honestly… it was scary.

Because by then, losing peace with you no longer felt small.

You mattered too much.

But this stage taught me something I’ll always keep.

Love isn’t proven when everything is easy.

It is proven when two people choose understanding over ego.

When they stay.

When they try again.

When they look at the mess and still say— “I want this.”

We were no longer two people enjoying good moments.

We had become two people learning how to protect them.

And maybe that was the moment I realised… I didn’t just love you during the beautiful parts.

I loved you enough to stay through the difficult ones too.
