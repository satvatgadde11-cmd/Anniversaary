STAGE 02-NOTICE


11th December.

Just another day in MSK-2 OPD.

She had newly joined her master’s journey, stepping into a new phase of life. I was a third-year student, standing at the edge of completing mine. Senior and junior. Two people connected only by work, patients, and case discussions. Our conversations stayed where they were supposed to stay — clinical reasoning, assessments, treatment plans, responsibilities.

Nothing more.

At least that’s what it looked like.

Because sometimes life doesn’t begin with grand entrances. Sometimes it quietly places two people in the same room and says nothing… while changing everything.

Back then, she was just another person in the OPD.

I just didn’t know she would become someone I’d remember long after those cases were over.
